How it works

1. Pick something

Start with the monthly picks or search all roles on Volunteering Dorset.

2. Sign up in one place

Click through to the role on Volunteering Dorset. Use the form there to register interest.

3. Turn up and enjoy it

You will get details from the organiser. If anything is unclear, Sue can help.

If a role is not on Volunteering Dorset it will not appear here. This keeps everything in one trusted place.
